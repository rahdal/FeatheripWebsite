About Us

Feathertip is an organization aimed to make graphic design more accessible by providing free graphic design services. Our focus is on small businesses and other organizations which would not be able to obtain quality graphic design under regular circumstances. Feathertip was founded in Southeast Michigan by Rahul Dalvi, a high school student.

Services

Feathertip is open to and offers a wide array of free graphic design services. From brochures to slideshow templates, Feathertip is happy to assist you in any way possible. Even if you feel as if your request is extremely odd, don't hesitate to reach out and see if we can fulfill your request. Feathertip can also provide any design and brand guidance that you need, all for free of charge.
